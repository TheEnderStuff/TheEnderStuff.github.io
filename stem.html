About The Project

The S.T.E.M Part Of Our Website Is Mostly Engineering And Tech Because Of What We Are Doing Which Is Building A Robotic Arm Or Two. We Will Be Using Coding, Soldering And Circuit Building. By Using Potentiometers, Servo Motors, Bread Boards And The Arduino Uno We Will Create A Fully Fuctional Robotic Arm.
